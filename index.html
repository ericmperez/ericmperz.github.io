Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

King Uniforms ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our King Uniforms Efficiency mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, role, and authentication credentials
  • Business Data: Client information, invoice data, product details, and order information
  • Maintenance Records: Machine data, maintenance tasks, photos, and completion records
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for pickup and delivery route tracking (only when using delivery features)
  • Photos: Images of machines, maintenance work, and equipment (stored with your explicit permission)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process and manage uniform cleaning orders and deliveries
  • Track and manage maintenance tasks and schedules
  • Send push notifications for maintenance reminders and task alerts
  • Optimize delivery routes and track pickup locations
  • Generate reports and analytics for business operations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Supabase: Cloud database with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Local Storage: Some data cached on your device for offline functionality
  • Cloud Storage: Photos and attachments stored in secure cloud storage buckets

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls based on user roles (Employee, Supervisor, Admin, Owner)
  • Automatic session timeout after inactivity

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • SendGrid: Email notification delivery
  • Firebase: Push notifications and analytics

5.2 Within Your Organization

  • Data is shared with authorized users within King Uniforms based on role permissions
  • Supervisors and Admins can view team activities and reports
  • Owners have full access to all business data

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in device settings
  • Location: Disable location services in device settings (may limit route tracking features)

7. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your camera and photo library to:

  • Capture photos of machines for maintenance records
  • Attach visual documentation to maintenance tasks
  • Upload equipment photos for tracking purposes

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings. Photos are only uploaded when you explicitly attach them to maintenance records.

8. Location Data

We collect location data only when you use delivery and route tracking features. Location data is used to:

  • Optimize pickup and delivery routes
  • Track driver locations for efficiency
  • Provide estimated arrival times

Location tracking can be disabled in device settings, though this will limit route tracking functionality.

9. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:

  • Maintenance task reminders at scheduled times
  • Overdue task alerts
  • Important system updates

You can manage notification preferences in the App's Settings or disable them entirely in your device settings.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Business Records: Financial and compliance data may be retained longer as required by law
  • Backup Data: Backups retained for 90 days for disaster recovery

12. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • Supabase: Privacy Policy
  • SendGrid: Privacy Policy
  • Firebase: Privacy Policy

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to registered users
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

15. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

16. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
